Ge0graphic wrote a review Nov 2016
Kolkata (Calcutta), India77 contributions44 helpful votes
The pacific resort Dhuturdaha, 50 kms from Kolkata
Traveled with family and friends on October 2016. Three families with kids went in personal vehicles. Around 45 kms from Science city along Basanti expressway.
Go straight around 35 kms from Science city along Basanti expressway then take right from Paglarghat bustop through Paglarghat Bodra road and ask for Dhuturdaha or just turn on the GPS and follow the map.
The resort is not a five star one but very much of 3 star category. Entrance is not impressive but the compound inside is refreshing. Rooms are clean and spacious with antique style furniture, split AC, cable TV and western toilet. A well maintained swimming pool with fountain is available to dive in. Got a big fish pond with angling facilities. I caught a big one. They can cook it or fry it upon request (complimentary). Food is sumptuous and abundant. There is also a beautiful riverside lawn just beside the compound wall, maintained by the same owner. A tree house is also in the making other than the main compound. The proprietor lives in the compound and is very cordial and down to earth. All the staffs are very attentive and service is prompt. Charges are 2200/room plus 800/head for food. Food includes tea/coffee, with morning and evening snacks (prawn fry/chicken fry/bhetki fry/onion fry), Lunch (Roti/rice, dal/vegetable of your choice, lobster or pompfret or Katla whichever you like, country chicken, Chatney/sweet), Dinner (Roti/rice, dal/vegetable of your choice, fish or country chicken or mutton, chatney/sweet). They don't charge for infants.
The resort is very refreshing and a perfect way to unwind for a day or two.
It is better to carry your choice of booze and cigarettes if you care. All brands are not available readily over there. We had a refreshing time in the swimming pool with chilled drinks served at the pool. If you like a calm and quite gateway amidst village, this is for you. If you are looking for a five star experience then forget it. As far as my family is concerned, we just loved it. Tips: Better if you go with atleast two family else the evening may be a little boring with pitch darkness around the compound. Will not make much difference though if you are a television fan (they have got a cable TV in each room).
